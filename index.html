A Sterile Sanctuary for New Life.

Globally, 30 to 40 million women go into labour without the care and safety of a hospital room and medical staff. Some even have to suffer through harsh conditions like extreme heat and air pollution. Unlike the home births that mothers may opt into in Canada. Worldwide, this usually isn't a choice that a mother makes, but rather one that is forced upon her. We are here to change that.

What is a BabyCave?

BabyCave is a tent-like structure. It provides a safe and sterile environment for about-to-be mothers to go through labour.

Key Features

🛏️

Cushioning

Soft, built-in cushioning for a comfortable labour experience.

🌡️

Temperature Detection

Identifies dangerous temperature conditions during labour.

💨

Ventilation

A vent to ensure breathability for the mother.
